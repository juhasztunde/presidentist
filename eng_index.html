Renáta Heintz, M.D

CEO of Presidentist


The relationship between the patient and the doctor is special. It is built on trust and it is very fragile. The demand for high quality can only be satisfied with precision matched with excellent expertise, however, as a physician, I believe empathy should be the keyword.

The most feasible solution can only be found if one pays careful attention. The unifacial resolutions could be cheaper however they are also heartless.

Our aim is to meet the unique requirement while preserving the old-fashioned human relations in a way that we can also keep up with the latest technologies. In our consulting rooms, we take care of our clients, who desire a healthy and beautiful set of teeth, using the most recent scientific possibilities and the latest disciplines of treatment.

How can we help?

Choose from our treatments based on your complaints

Fillings

broken tooth, discolouration, cold/warm/bite sensitivity, pain, unpleasant breath

Periodontology

gums bleeding, dental plaque, gums retraction, moving teeth, swollen/red gums, unpleasant breath

Root canal treatment

tooth pain, broken tooth, bite sensitivity, discolouration, cyst/ abscess, unpleasant breath

Implants

lack of teeth, irreparable teeth, total toothless, orthodontic treatments

Oral surgery

abnormal wisdom tooth, impacted teeth, irreparable tooth, cyst/abscess, bone replacement, mucosal lesions

Dental restorations

tooth deficiency, aesthetic problem, jaw joint clicking/pain, aged replacements, unpleasant breath

Orthodontics

irregular position of teeth (congestion/rotation/too large gaps), tooth deficiency, bite disorders, aesthetic problem, a solution without the extraction of teeth, for adults too
