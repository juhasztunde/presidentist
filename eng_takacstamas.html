Dr. Tamás Takács


“Work is truly a joy if it is creative. Fortunately, dentistry provides the challenge every day to create something beautiful, tailored to an individual. I have patience and determination as a base, the patient only needs to join in to my commitment."

I graduated from the Faculty of Dentistry at the University of Szeged in 2011. Even during my university studies I developed a fascination for aesthetic dentistry, and that didn’t deplete until the present day. I have participated in aesthetic competitions achieving second place in 2010. From the beginning I have been working in practices where foreign patients were treated as part of so-called dental tourism. For this reason it has always been extremely important to me to become fully experienced in the field of implantology at the highest level possible. Therefore, in 2014/15 I took an accredited implantology course at Münster University and obtained a Master’s Degree in Implantology.
