Dr. Georgina Michailovits


“The most important principle of the medical profession is the preservation of health. This is the purpose of periodontology, which, unlike other areas of dentistry, focuses primarily on the preservation of teeth rather than their replacement. That is why I chose it as my profession, and my goal is to convince people that their teeth can be kept for the rest of their lives. It is my job to show them how to achieve this and use my medical work to help restore my patients’ oral health, which also leads them on the path of preserving their general health."

I obtained my degree in dentistry at the University of Szeged’s Faculty of Dentistry in 2014, after moving to Budapest, I started working at the Periodontology Clinic of Semmelweis University. I took my medical bar exam in periodontology in 2017. Since then I have been working full-time as a specialist at the Periodontology Clinic of Semmelweis University.

I have been a member of the Presidentist team since the beginning of 2015, treating patients with periodontal problems. Besides that, I help my Colleagues in aesthetic rehabilitation.
