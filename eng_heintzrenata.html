Dr. Renáta Heinz


"I believe myself lucky. Few people can say they take great pleasure in their work. Through my work in oral surgery, I feel I have gained the affection and satisfaction of patients, some of whom thought they were in hopeless situations. Building on this source of joy I am widening my knowledge open to the latest technology and methods so that patients can get to the result they desire in the least complicated way: an exhilarated smile,

Dr. Renáta Heinz, has over 20 years’ experience in implantology and oral surgery. Since the first years of her career, she has been participating in teaching at the Dental and Oral Surgery Clinic of Albert Szent-Györgyi Medical University.

She has been a member of the management of the Hungarian Implantology Association for three cycles and expanded her knowledge of Implantology on international trips.

She was recently a full-time, now is a part-time associate of the Department of Oral And Maxillofacial Surgery established in 2008, and participates in postgraduate student training, as well as being a lecturer of the accredited MSc Implantology course at Münster University.

Dr Heintz is a member of the International Team of Implantology, and has been in private practice since 1995; she is currently the chief doctor at the Presidentist dental clinic. Having outstanding professional experience in implant surgery, dental care and in the surgical solutions of bone deficits, she participates as an expert in complex implant cases at more than one practice in Hungary. The team of dentists she leads provides high-quality and full-range services in all areas of dentistry.
