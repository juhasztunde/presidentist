Renáta Heinz, M.D.


"I feel lucky. Few people can say they find the joys of everyday life in their work. In my oral surgery, I gained the love and satisfaction of patients, often after their hopeless situation. I'm building my knowledge on this source of joy, open to the latest technologies and methods so that patients can find the most gentle, easy way to reach their desired joyous smile."

Renáta Heinz, M.D, has over 20 years of experience in dental implant and oral surgery. From the first years of her career, she participated in the Dental and Oral Surgery Clinic of Albert Szent-Györgyi Medical University.

She has been a member of the management for three cycles of the Hungarian Implantology Association. She expanded her knowledge of Implantology on international study trips.

She’s been a full-time and later part-time associate of the Facial, Jaw and Oral Surgery Clinic of the University of Szeged, established in 2008. She participates in postgraduate student training and is the lecturer of the accredited M.Sc. Implantology course at Münster University.

She’s a member of the International Team of Implantology. Dr. Heintz has also been in private practice since 1995, she’s currently the chief doctor of the Presidentist dental clinic. She has excellent professional experience in implant surgery, dental care and in the surgical treatment of bone deficits. She participates as an expert when it comes to complicated implant cases in more than one practice in Hungary. The team of dentists led by her provides high-quality and full-range services in all areas of dentistry.
