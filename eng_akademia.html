Tooth brushing academy

The majority of our patients strive with determination to achieve the correct way of brushing their teeth. What could possibly explain the fact that dentists still find something wrong?

We, doctors, often feel that patients are reluctant to talk about this topic. They skim over it by saying they brush their teeth many times a day, even after every meal, so in their opinion, there shouldn’t be anything wrong. When we give advice, they sometimes react with resentment because they cannot imagine how much good technique matters. They derive their problems from their inherited wrong tooth matter. However, the reality is that even the worst situation can be corrected and a better condition can be preserved for a long time, even for life. Nonetheless, an electric toothbrush is not enough, even if it is paired with the most expensive toothpaste. The world is changing, our knowledge and opportunities are growing in every field. Tooth brushing is not an exception either.

The vast majority of dental diseases can simply be prevented by correct brushing techniques. In our Tooth Brushing Academy program, our patients are introduced to the latest methods, which if applied can result in achieving excellent oral hygiene. In a different dental situation such as the irregular position of teeth, bridges and implants, we do this with different tools. During the meeting, after a professional cleaning, we provide personalized advice on the use of new cleaning tools and give an opportunity to also try them out.
